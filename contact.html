Home Price Map is a solo, independent project, and feedback genuinely shapes what gets built next. Whether you've found a number that looks wrong, have an idea for a feature, want to understand how something is calculated, or have a question about advertising or the data, the best way to reach the site is the contact form below.

Good reasons to get in touch

A few things people most often write in about:

Data corrections. If a home value, crime rate, or income figure looks off for a place you know well, let me know which county or city and what looks wrong. Every figure traces back to a public source, so most surprises have an explanation — but genuine mistakes do happen, and they get fixed.

Feature requests. The site is actively growing. Recent additions like crime data, household income, and affordability rankings all started as suggestions. If there's something you wish you could see on the map, it's worth asking.

Methodology questions. If you want to understand exactly how a specific number is calculated before relying on it, the Data & Methodology page covers most of it, but you're welcome to ask about anything it doesn't.
