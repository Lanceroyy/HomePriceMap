Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Home Price Map ("we," "this site") is a free tool for exploring publicly available U.S. home price data. This page explains what data is collected when you visit and how it's used.

What this site does not do

We don't have user accounts, don't collect names, addresses, or payment information, and don't sell any data to third parties. There are no forms on this site that collect personal information.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns — which pages are visited, how long people stay, and general geographic/device information. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects data such as your IP address (in anonymized/truncated form), browser type, and pages viewed. This data is processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

Advertising

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Cookies

Cookies set by this site are used solely for analytics and advertising as described above. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time; the site will continue to function normally without them, though analytics and ad personalization will be affected.

Data source

The home price data shown on this site is sourced from Zillow Research (Zillow Home Value Index) and the U.S. Census Bureau's Gazetteer Files, both of which are publicly available. This site is not affiliated with or endorsed by Zillow or the U.S. Census Bureau.
