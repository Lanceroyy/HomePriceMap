Frequently asked questions about Hawaii home prices

Which county in Hawaii has the most expensive homes?

Kauai County has the highest median home value in Hawaii at $1,024,959. This is often searched as the "richest" or "wealthiest" county in Hawaii — though it's worth being precise about what's being measured here: median home value reflects property prices, not household income or net worth. The two usually correlate, but a county can have expensive homes without having the highest-earning residents, particularly in areas with a lot of vacation or second homes.

Which county in Hawaii has the highest household income?

Honolulu County has the highest median household income in Hawaii at $106,195. If what you're really after is the "richest" or "wealthiest" county in Hawaii, this is the closer answer — income measures what households actually earn each year, while home values measure what property costs. Neither is quite the same as wealth, which would also account for savings, investments and property people already own outright.

What is the cheapest county in Hawaii?

Hawaii County has the lowest median home value of the 4 Hawaii counties tracked here, at $576,247. Keep in mind that low home prices often reflect limited local job markets, distance from major metro areas, or shrinking population — affordability alone doesn't tell you whether somewhere is a good fit.
